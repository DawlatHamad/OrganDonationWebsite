How old do I have to be to sign up?

A person can register as a donor at the age of 18. For persons under the age of 18, a parent or guardian needs to grant the donor permission. There is no limit regarding the age of a donor, as long as they meet medical conditions.

How do I sign up to be a donor?

You can sign up thru an organization or at your state's motor vehicle offfice

Will being a donor effect the medical care that I recieve?

No, when admitted for medical treatment your medical team will do the best they can for your recovery
