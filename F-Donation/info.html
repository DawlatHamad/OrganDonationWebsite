Checking Donor Status

Adding your name to the Michigan Organ Donor Registry is very simple. This can be done when you are applying or renewing a driver's license.

Where to look:

  • Check for the red heart symbol at the bottom corner of license
  • If not, fill out the Donor Registry form
  • Request a new license which will now include the red heart symbol

Age

There is no age limit for those that can register as donors. People under the age of 18 can become donors, however a legal guardian can revoke that decision. Be sure to dicuss your plans to become a donor with family regardless of age.

What you can Donate

Posted by Gift of Life, May 26, 2018

A person is capable of donating more than just organs. The corneas, tissue, limbs, blood and platelets can be donated. This part will only provide information on just the organs thta a person can donate.

Donate while Alive

Kidney:
One of a pair of organs in the abdomen. Removes waste and extra water from the blood and help keep chemicals balanced in the body. 1
Lung:
One of a pair of organs in the chest that supplies the body with oxygen, and removes carbon dioxide from the body. 1
Liver -- only part of:
A large lobed glandular organ in the abdomen of vertebrates, involved in many metabolic processes.
