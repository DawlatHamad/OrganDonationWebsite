Lecture

Posted by Gift of Life, June 06, 2023

For the next month, this organization will hold weekly meetings. They will go over the process of organ donation and the circumstances that lead to donations. The highlighted topic will feature Circulatory Death! Circulatory Death occurs when the heart stops and the person cannot be resuscitated. In Rajab and Singh's article, Donation After Cardiac Death, circulatory death is described as Death of DCD donors is declared on the basis of irreversible cardiac arrest rather than irreversible loss of all functions of the entire brain.
